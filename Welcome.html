RULES

WEIGHT LIMITS VARY


WRAP all mattresses and box springs in plastic (no tarps) before placing at the curb for bulk waste collection.

DO NOT place material on top of snow banks.

Learn more about Waste Management Site hours and location and Waste Management Site fees.

DO NOT put NEEDLES, SYRINGES or LANCETS in your Garbage, Blue Box or Green Cart. Contact your pharmacy or doctor's office for proper disposal advice.

Wrap broken glass and sharp objects before putting them in the garbage.

REMOVE LINERS from tissue boxes, and put the liners in the garbage

TIE corrugated cardboard in bundles

DRY OUT, REMOVE LIDS, and EMPTY OUT paint cans before recycling
